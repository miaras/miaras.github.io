  Disability
  What is a disability? Blindness is a disability. Fibromyalgia is a disability. Deafness is a disability. I think there are many kinds of disabilities.
  Disabilities may make life more difficult. If I was blind, I would bump into things and objects. But I do not have to think that my disability makes my experience of life less valuable. My experience of life is different because of my disability. But it is not less valuable.
