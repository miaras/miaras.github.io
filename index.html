MIARA SUNG (they/them) is a Korean/American writer and programmer based in Amsterdam. Their writing has appeared in Tropics of Meta, Centre for Experimental Ontology, and is forthcoming in Abolishing Capitalist Totality, a collection by Archive Books. Formerly active in the Seattle literary scene, they performed poetry at Lit Crawl Seattle and was invited to perform at Hugo House.

Miara has previously worked on search, literary machine translation and digital literature platforms. They are currently working on language and humanities education platforms for solo learners, institutions, and distributed communities.
