MIARA SUNG (they/them) is a Korean/American writer and technologist based in Amsterdam. Their writing has appeared in Tropics of Meta, Centre for Experimental Ontology, and is forthcoming in Abolishing Capitalist Totality, a collection by Archive Books. Formerly active in the Seattle literary scene, they performed poetry at Lit Crawl Seattle and was invited to perform at Hugo House.

Miara studied computer science and philosophy at UC Berkeley, where they taught a course on philosophy of computation. They have worked as a software engineer at Microsoft and as a researcher funded by the Ethereum community. They also made the indie game 6180 the moon with some friends.
